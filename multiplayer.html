Multiplayer Experience

Compete in intense multiplayer matches across various modes and maps.

Game Modes

Team Deathmatch

Classic 6v6 or 12v12 team elimination. First team to reach 75 kills wins!

Play Time: 10-15 minutes

Search & Destroy

Plant the bomb or defend against attackers. Strategic gameplay required.

Play Time: 15-20 minutes

Domination

Control three flags to earn points. First to 200 points wins!

Play Time: 12-18 minutes

Free For All

Every player for themselves. Last one standing wins!

Play Time: 8-12 minutes

Popular Maps

Crash

Urban warfare in a crashed plane facility. Great for all playstyles.

Rust

Small, intense map. Perfect for close-quarters combat!

Nuketown

Classic fan-favorite. Fast-paced action guaranteed!
